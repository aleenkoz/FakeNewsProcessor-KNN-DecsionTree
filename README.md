# FakeNewsProcessor-KNN-DecsionTree
Nowadays, most people read the news through social media and online websites. Because of this, 
fake or misleading news can spread very quickly. Many users do not have enough time or tools to 
check whether the information is true, which may affect their opinions and decisions. For this 
reason, it is useful to build a system that can help detect fake news automatically.
The problem in this project is to classify news articles as real or fake using features taken from 
the text. These features may include word patterns, frequency of certain terms, or other simple 
indicators that reflect the reliability of the content.
To solve this problem, two machine learning algorithms are used: Decision Tree and K-Nearest Neighbors 
(KNN). The Decision Tree creates classification rules by splitting the data based on 
important features. In contrast, KNN compares a new article with similar articles in the dataset and 
assigns the most common label among its nearest neighbors.
